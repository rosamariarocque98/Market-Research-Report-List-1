<p><p>Machine Direction Orientation (MDO) systems are classified into three main types: horizontal, vertical, and others. Horizontal MDO systems operate with the material moving through the equipment horizontally, providing efficient stretch and orientation. Vertical MDO systems, on the other hand, work with the material oriented upright, facilitating effective space utilization and material handling. Other market types may include customized designs or hybrid systems that combine features of both horizontal and vertical orientations to meet specific production needs.</p></p>
