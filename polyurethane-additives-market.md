<p><p>Polyurethane additives are specialized chemicals used to enhance the performance, durability, and processing of polyurethane materials. <p><p>The Polyurethane Additives market includes key types like Catalysts, Surfactants, and Fillers. Catalysts accelerate the chemical reactions in polyurethane formation, enhancing curing and processing efficiency. Surfactants stabilize foam structures, ensuring uniformity and improving cell formation in flexible and rigid foams. Fillers are added to modify properties like strength, durability, and thermal conductivity, often reducing costs. Together, these additives optimize polyurethane performance across industries such as construction, automotive, and furniture, catering to diverse application needs.</p></p>
