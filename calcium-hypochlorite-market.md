<p><ul><li>Calcium Process</li><li>Sodium Process</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Calcium Hypochlorite market is primarily segmented into two production processes: the Calcium Process and the Sodium Process. The Calcium Process involves the reaction of calcium hydroxide with chlorine gas, resulting in a high concentration of calcium hypochlorite, commonly used for water treatment and disinfection. In contrast, the Sodium Process utilizes sodium hypochlorite as a precursor, offering a different composition and properties. Both processes cater to various applications, including public health, swimming pools, and industrial cleaning, impacting market dynamics and demand.</p></p>
