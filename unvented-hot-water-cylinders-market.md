<p><ul><li>Direct Heating</li><li>Indirect Heating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The unvented hot water cylinders market comprises two main types: direct heating and indirect heating systems. Direct heating involves an integral electric element that heats the water directly within the cylinder, providing immediate hot water access. In contrast, indirect heating utilizes an external heat source, such as a boiler, to heat the water, which is then stored in the cylinder. Each type has its advantages, with direct heating offering quick response times and indirect heating enabling more efficient use of existing heating systems.</p></p>
