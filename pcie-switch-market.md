<p><p>The PCIe switch market comprises multiple generations, each offering enhanced performance and bandwidth. PCIe 2.0 delivers 5 GT/s, suitable for basic applications. PCIe 3.0, with 8 GT/s, improves throughput for more demanding tasks. PCIe 4.0 doubles that to 16 GT/s, supporting high-speed data transfer in enterprise solutions. PCIe 5.0 reaches 32 GT/s, enabling advanced workloads like AI and machine learning. PCIe 6.0, the latest, pushes this further to 64 GT/s, addressing the growing needs of data-intensive applications and next-gen computing.</p></p>
