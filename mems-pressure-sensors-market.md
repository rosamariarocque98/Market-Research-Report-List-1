<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The MEMS Pressure Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Capacitive Pressure Sensors</li><li>Piezoresistive Pressure Sensors</li><li>Piezoelectric Pressure Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEMS pressure sensors market includes various types, each leveraging distinct operational principles. Capacitive pressure sensors measure pressure through changes in capacitance due to diaphragm deflection. Piezoresistive pressure sensors utilize the piezoresistive effect, where resistance changes with applied pressure, making them sensitive and widely used. Piezoelectric pressure sensors generate an electrical charge in response to mechanical stress, ideal for dynamic pressure measurements. These technologies cater to diverse applications, including automotive, industrial, and medical sectors, driven by their accuracy and miniaturization.</p></p>
