<p><p>The Camera Module & Touch Sensor Assembly Adhesives market is segmented into two main types: UV Curable and Non-UV Curable adhesives. UV Curable adhesives utilize ultraviolet light to rapidly cure and bond materials, offering quick processing times and strong adhesion, ideal for high-precision applications in camera and touch sensor assemblies. In contrast, Non-UV Curable adhesives cure through heat or moisture, providing versatile bonding options for various substrates, particularly where UV exposure is impractical. Both types cater to diverse manufacturing needs in electronics.</p></p>
