<p><ul><li>Suspension</li><li>Colloid</li><li>Solution</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The **dispersant market** is categorized into **suspension**, **colloid**, **solution**, and **powder** types. **Suspension dispersants** stabilize solid particles in liquids, preventing settling. **Colloid dispersants** maintain fine particles uniformly in a medium, enhancing stability. **Solution dispersants** dissolve completely in liquids, ensuring homogeneous mixtures. **Powder dispersants** are solid forms that disperse upon mixing, ideal for dry applications. Each type serves specific industries, such as paints, coatings, pharmaceuticals, and agriculture, by improving product performance, stability, and consistency.</p></p>
