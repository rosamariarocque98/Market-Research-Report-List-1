<p><p>The automotive transmission oil pump market is primarily divided into mechanical and electric oil pumps. Mechanical oil pumps utilize engine power to circulate transmission fluid, offering reliability and simplicity, making them popular in traditional vehicles. Electric oil pumps, on the other hand, are powered by the vehicle's electrical system, providing enhanced efficiency and adaptability for modern transmissions, particularly in hybrid and electric vehicles. Each type caters to different vehicle designs and performance requirements, shaping their respective market roles.</p></p>
