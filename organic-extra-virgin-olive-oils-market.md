<p><strong>The Organic Extra Virgin Olive Oils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PDO / DOP</li><li>PGI / IGP</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic extra virgin olive oil market is categorized into several types based on regional and production standards. PDO (Protected Designation of Origin) or DOP (Denominazione di Origine Protetta) certifies oils from specific regions, ensuring traditional production methods. PGI (Protected Geographical Indication) or IGP (Indicazione Geografica Protetta) signifies oils with qualities linked to their geographical origin but allows more flexibility in processing. Other markets may include generic organic oils without specific regional ties, focusing on broader organic certification standards.</p></p>
