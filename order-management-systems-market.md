<p><p>Order Management Systems (OMS) can be categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise OMS are installed locally on a company's servers, offering control and customization but requiring significant IT resources and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based OMS are hosted on remote servers, providing scalability, flexibility, and lower upfront costs, as they are subscription-based. Businesses choose based on their needs, with cloud options often preferred for ease of updates and access from anywhere, while on-premise systems cater to specific security or compliance requirements.</p></p>
