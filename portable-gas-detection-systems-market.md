<p><p>The Portable Gas Detection Systems market includes several types of technologies. Semiconductor sensors detect gases based on changes in electrical conductivity, while electrochemical sensors use chemical reactions to measure gas concentrations. Catalytic combustion sensors identify flammable gases through combustion, whereas photoionization detectors (PID) utilize ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules for detection. Lastly, infrared sensors detect specific gases by measuring their absorption of infrared light. Each type serves distinct applications and industries, contributing to workplace safety and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
