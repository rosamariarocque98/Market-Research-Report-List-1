<p><strong>The Medical Perfusion System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cardiopulmonary Perfusion</li><li>Cell Perfusion</li><li>Ex Vivo Organ Perfusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical perfusion system market encompasses three primary types: cardiopulmonary perfusion, cell perfusion, and ex vivo organ perfusion. Cardiopulmonary perfusion is crucial during heart surgeries, providing oxygenated blood to the body. Cell perfusion focuses on supplying nutrients and oxygen to cells for research and therapeutic purposes, enhancing cell viability. Ex vivo organ perfusion involves preserving and maintaining organs outside the body for transplantation, improving organ viability and function until transplantation. Each type plays a vital role in modern medical practices.</p></p>
