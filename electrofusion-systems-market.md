<p><p>Electrofusion systems are a method of joining thermoplastic pipes using an electrofusion welding process, where an electric current is passed through a coil embedded in the fitting. <p><strong>The Electrofusion Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC Waveform</li><li>DC Waveform</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electrofusion Systems market is primarily categorized into AC Waveform and DC Waveform types. AC Waveform systems use alternating current to generate heat through resistance, enabling pipe fusion, particularly in larger diameter applications. In contrast, DC Waveform systems employ direct current, providing a more controlled heating process, which leads to precise fusion in smaller diameter pipes. Each waveform type offers unique advantages, influencing their suitability for various applications in industries such as plumbing, gas distribution, and wastewater management.</p></p>
