<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Ophthalmic Ultrasound System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-Scan</li><li>B-Scan</li><li>Combined Scan</li><li>Pachymetry</li><li>Ultrasound Bio Microscope (UBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ophthalmic ultrasound system market encompasses various types, each serving distinct diagnostic purposes. A-Scan provides precise axial measurements of the eye, crucial for cataract surgery. B-Scan offers two-dimensional imaging, helping visualize ocular structures, including tumors. Combined Scan integrates A and B-Scan functionalities for comprehensive assessments. Pachymetry measures corneal thickness, vital for glaucoma management. Lastly, the Ultrasound Bio Microscope (UBM) delivers high-resolution imaging of anterior segment structures, enhancing the diagnosis of conditions like anterior chamber abnormalities.</p></p>
