<p><p>The silica extinction powder market comprises different types, primarily categorized as precipitated silica, meteorological method silica, and gel method silica. Precipitated silica is produced through a chemical reaction, offering high purity and specific surface areas, making it suitable for various applications such as rubber and cosmetics. Meteorological method silica is derived from atmospheric processes, emphasizing environmental responsiveness. Gel method silica, produced from silica gels, offers unique properties for use in pharmaceuticals and electronics. These variations cater to diverse industrial needs and applications.</p></p>
