<p><p>The half-bridge driver market is categorized into single channel and dual channel types, each serving distinct applications. A single channel half-bridge driver controls one half-bridge circuit, typically used in simpler applications like small motors or basic power management. In contrast, dual channel drivers can control two half-bridge circuits simultaneously, making them suitable for more complex systems such as robotics and high-performance motor drives. The choice between single and dual channels often depends on the required power efficiency and control complexity.</p></p>
