<p><p>The Passenger Boarding Stairs market includes two primary types: Towable Passenger Stairways and Chassis Mounted Passenger Stairways. Towable Passenger Stairways are mobile units that can be connected to a vehicle for easy transport to different locations, making them versatile for various airport settings. In contrast, Chassis Mounted Passenger Stairways are integrated into a dedicated vehicle designed for boarding passengers directly at the aircraft, offering stability and efficiency during operations. Both types enhance passenger accessibility and streamline boarding processes.</p></p>
