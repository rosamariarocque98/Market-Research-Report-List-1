<p><p>The Automation Pipette Tips market is divided into two main types: non-filtered pipette tips and filtered pipette tips. Non-filtered tips are designed for routine sample transfer, providing high precision without barriers to fluid passage. In contrast, filtered pipette tips incorporate a barrier that prevents aerosol contamination, making them ideal for handling volatile or sensitive samples. Both types enhance laboratory efficiency, but the choice depends on the specific contamination control needs and application requirements of the laboratory.</p></p>
