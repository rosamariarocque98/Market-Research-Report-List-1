<p><p>Bone wax is a sterile barrier material used in orthopedic and neurosurgical procedures to control bleeding from bone surfaces. <p><p>The Bone Wax market is categorized into absorbable and non-absorbable types. Absorbable Bone Wax is designed to be gradually absorbed by the body, minimizing the need for surgical removal and reducing complications related to foreign materials. Non-absorbable Bone Wax, on the other hand, provides immediate hemostatic control but must be surgically removed if it causes issues or complications later. Both types are utilized in orthopedic and neurosurgical procedures to manage bone bleeding and ensure optimal healing outcomes.</p></p>
