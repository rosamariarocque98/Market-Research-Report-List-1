Rubber accelerators expedite the vulcanization process, improving production efficiency and rubber properties. Other additives encompass a range of chemicals that modify rubber's characteristics, such as fillers, processing aids, and antiozonants. Together, these components play a critical role in optimizing the performance and durability of rubber materials.</p></p>
