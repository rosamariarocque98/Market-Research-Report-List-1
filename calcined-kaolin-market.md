<p><p>The calcined kaolin market features various types, with two significant segments being absorbent and inhibitory. Absorbent calcined kaolin is used primarily in applications requiring moisture absorption, such as in paints, coatings, and plastics. Its ability to enhance durability and performance makes it valuable in these sectors. In contrast, the inhibitory market focuses on calcined kaolin's role in preventing unwanted reactions, such as corrosion or microbial growth, often utilized in pharmaceuticals, food packaging, and personal care products for added safety and stability.</p></p>
