<p><p>The **Paver (Vehicle) Market** includes **Mechanical Pavers** and **Hydrostatic Pavers**, both used for road construction. **Mechanical Pavers** rely on mechanical transmissions, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness, ideal for smaller projects. **Hydrostatic Pavers** use hydraulic systems for smoother operation, precise control, and higher efficiency, suitable for large-scale, high-precision tasks. While mechanical pavers are durable and easier to maintain, hydrostatic pavers provide advanced performance and adaptability, catering to diverse construction needs.
