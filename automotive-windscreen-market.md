<p><p>The automotive windscreen market comprises several types of glass, primarily laminated glass and toughened glass. Laminated glass is made of two or more layers of glass held together by a transparent interlayer, providing safety by preventing shattering. Toughened glass, also known as tempered glass, is heated and cooled rapidly to increase strength, shattering into small, less harmful pieces upon impact. Other types may include hybrid or additional specialty glasses designed for enhanced resistance to environmental factors or advanced functionality.</p></p>
