<p><p>The Metal Degreaser market comprises two main types: Organic Solvent and Aqueous Cleaner. Organic Solvent degreasers use chemical solvents like hydrocarbons or chlorinated compounds to dissolve oils, greases, and contaminants, offering fast evaporation and strong cleaning power, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Aqueous Cleaners, on the other hand, are water-based solutions containing detergents or surfactants, providing eco-friendly, non-flammable, and safer alternatives, suitable for lighter cleaning tasks and industries prioritizing environmental compliance and worker safety. Both cater to diverse industrial cleaning needs.</p></p>
