<p><p>Foamed plastics insulation products encompass various types, each serving unique applications. Polyurethane foam offers high thermal resistance and versatility, making it popular in construction. Polystyrene, available in expanded and extruded forms, is lightweight and moisture-resistant, suitable for walls and roofs. Polyisocyanurate boasts superior R-values, often used in commercial buildings. Phenolic foam is known for its low thermal conductivity and fire resistance, ideal for industrial settings. Other materials may include bio-based foams and specialty formulations tailored for specific environments.</p></p>
