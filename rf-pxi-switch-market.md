<p><ul><li>SPDT switch</li><li>Double pole double throw switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF PXI switch market features various types of switches, including Single Pole Double Throw (SPDT) switches and Double Pole Double Throw (DPDT) switches. SPDT switches enable the routing of a single signal to one of two outputs, making them ideal for applications requiring signal selection. In contrast, DPDT switches manage two separate signals simultaneously, allowing for more complex configurations. Both types are crucial in test and measurement applications, facilitating efficient signal management and improving overall system performance.</p></p>
