<p><p>Electric pet fence systems are primarily categorized into two types: in-ground fences and wireless fences. In-ground fences involve burying a wire around the property's perimeter, creating a controlled boundary that triggers a collar when pets attempt to cross it. Conversely, wireless fences use a transmitter to establish a circular boundary without any physical wires. Both systems provide pet owners with effective solutions to contain their animals, enhancing safety while allowing pets the freedom to roam within designated areas.</p></p>
