<p><p>The Glucose Responsive Insulin System market comprises various insulin types tailored for diabetes management. Intermediate-acting insulins cover longer periods for basal needs, while long-acting insulins ensure sustained glucose control over 24 hours. Premixed types combine short- and intermediate-acting formulations in one, providing convenience for patients needing both rapid and extended coverage in their treatment regimen.</p></p>
