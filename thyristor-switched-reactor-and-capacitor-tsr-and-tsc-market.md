<p><p>Thyristor Switched Reactors (TSR) and Thyristor Switched Capacitors (TSC) are advanced power electronic devices used for dynamic reactive power management in electrical power systems. TSRs are employed to absorb excess reactive power, while TSCs supply reactive power, improving voltage stability and mitigating issues related to power quality. These technologies enhance the efficiency of power systems by providing rapid and precise control of reactive power, which is essential for maintaining system reliability and performance.</p><p>The market for TSR and TSC is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for stable and efficient power supply systems, especially in regions with expanding industrial and commercial sectors.
