<p><ul><li>Multi-leaf Springs</li><li>Mono-leaf Springs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile sheet spring assembly market includes multi-leaf and mono-leaf springs, both used for vehicle suspension. Multi-leaf springs consist of multiple steel layers stacked together, offering durability and load-bearing capacity, ideal for heavy-duty vehicles like trucks. Mono-leaf springs use a single, thicker steel layer, providing a lighter, more compact design suitable for modern passenger cars, enhancing ride comfort and fuel efficiency. Both types balance performance, weight, and cost, catering to diverse automotive needs, from commercial to personal vehicles.</p></p>
