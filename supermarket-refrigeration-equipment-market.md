<p><p>The supermarket refrigeration equipment market is divided into two main types: plug-in and remote refrigeration systems. Plug-in units are self-contained, easy to install, and typically used for smaller applications, offering flexibility for retailers. Remote refrigeration systems, on the other hand, utilize centralized cooling systems that are more energy-efficient and are often preferred for larger setups. These systems are ideal for extensive displays or large storage areas, promoting consistent temperature control and optimized energy use throughout the supermarket.</p></p>
