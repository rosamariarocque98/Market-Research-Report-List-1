<p><p>The subsea flowlines market is categorized into three main types based on operating pressure: low pressure, medium pressure, and high pressure. Low pressure flowlines typically handle pressures up to 1,500 psi, suited for shallow offshore fields or low-density fluids. Medium pressure flowlines, ranging from 1,500 to 5,000 psi, cater to moderate-depth fields with more varied fluid characteristics. High pressure flowlines operate above 5,000 psi, designed for deepwater applications requiring robust materials to manage extreme conditions and greater fluid transport efficiency.</p></p>
