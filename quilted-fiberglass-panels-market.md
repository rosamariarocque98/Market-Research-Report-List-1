<p><p>Quilted fiberglass panels are available in two primary market types: one-sided and double-sided. One-sided panels feature a fiberglass layer on only one side, making them lightweight and cost-effective for applications where aesthetics are less critical. In contrast, double-sided panels have fiberglass layers on both sides, enhancing insulation and acoustic properties while providing a more polished appearance. These panels cater to various needs, including industrial settings, climate control, and interior finishes, depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
