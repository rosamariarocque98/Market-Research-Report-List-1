<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment (EVSE) market includes DC and AC charging systems. DC charging, known as fast or rapid charging, delivers high-power direct current directly to the EV battery, enabling quicker charging times, ideal for public stations and long-distance travel. AC charging, typically slower, uses alternating current converted to DC by the vehicle's onboard charger, suited for home or workplace use. Both systems cater to diverse charging needs, balancing speed, convenience, and infrastructure requirements for EV adoption.</p></p>
