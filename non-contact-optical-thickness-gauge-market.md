<p><p>The Non-Contact Optical Thickness Gauge market is segmented into two primary types: Halogen Light Source and Infrared Laser Diode (LD). The Halogen Light Source utilizes broad-spectrum light to measure thickness, offering high resolution and reliability for various materials. In contrast, the Infrared LD employs focused infrared light that enhances precision and sensitivity, particularly for thin films and delicate surfaces. Both types cater to diverse industries, ensuring accurate measurements without physical contact, thus preventing surface damage.</p></p>
