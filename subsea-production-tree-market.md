<p><p>The subsea production tree market is divided into two primary types: vertical subsea trees and horizontal subsea trees. Vertical subsea trees feature a design that allows for straight vertical access to the wellhead, making them suitable for deepwater applications. In contrast, horizontal subsea trees are designed for installations with space constraints or complex seabed configurations, providing flexibility in layout and connections. Both types play a crucial role in the efficient extraction of oil and gas from underwater reservoirs.</p></p>
