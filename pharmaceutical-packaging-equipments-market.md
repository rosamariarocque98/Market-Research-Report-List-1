<p><p>The pharmaceutical packaging equipment market comprises various segments. Primary packaging equipment focuses on containing the product directly, like bottles, blister packs, and vials. Secondary packaging equipment handles the group packaging, including cartons and boxes that protect and organize multiple primary packages. Labeling and serialization equipment ensures compliance and traceability by applying labels with essential information and unique identifiers, which are critical for regulatory mandates and supply chain integrity. Together, these types enhance product safety and facilitate efficient distribution.</p></p>
