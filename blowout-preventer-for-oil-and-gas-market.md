<p><p>Blowout Preventers (BOPs) are critical safety devices in the oil and gas industry, designed to control and seal well pressures during drilling. The market is primarily categorized into two types: Annular Blowout Preventers, which use a rubber-like sealing element to close around a drill pipe or seal the well completely, and Ram Blowout Preventers, which utilize movable metal blocks to shear through pipe or seal the well. Both ensure safety and prevent uncontrolled releases of oil and gas.</p></p>
