<p><p>The pressure-reducing (boost) valve market primarily includes two types: pressure-reducing valves and boost valves. Pressure-reducing valves manage and lower incoming pressure to a specified output level, ensuring safe and efficient system operation. Boost valves, on the other hand, increase pressure to meet specific system demands or enhance fluid delivery in applications where pressure is insufficient. Both types are essential for maintaining optimal performance in various industries, including water distribution, HVAC systems, and industrial processes.</p></p>
