<p><p>Terminal LCD displays are categorized primarily into two types: TFT (Thin Film Transistor) and Monographic LCD displays. TFT LCDs offer vibrant colors and high-resolution images, making them ideal for applications requiring detailed graphics, such as smartphones and monitors. In contrast, Monographic LCDs provide simpler, often monochrome displays suitable for devices like calculators and basic information panels, where low power consumption and cost-effectiveness are essential. Both types cater to different market needs, impacting their design and application across various industries.</p></p>
