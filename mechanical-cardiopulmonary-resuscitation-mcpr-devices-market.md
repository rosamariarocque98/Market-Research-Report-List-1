<p><p>The mechanical cardiopulmonary resuscitation (mCPR) devices market is divided into two main types: electric and pneumatic. Electric mCPR devices operate using electric motors to automate chest compressions, providing consistent and adjustable pressure levels to enhance blood circulation during cardiac events. Pneumatic mCPR devices, on the other hand, utilize compressed air to generate mechanical movements for chest compressions. Both types aim to deliver effective resuscitation while minimizing the need for manual intervention, improving patient outcomes in emergency situations.</p></p>
