<p><strong>The Non invasive Intracranial Pressure Monitoring Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transcranial Doppler Ultrasonography</li><li>Tympanic Membrane Displacement (TMD)</li><li>Optic Nerve Sheath Diameter</li><li>MRI/CT</li><li>Fundoscopy (Papilledema)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-invasive Intracranial Pressure Monitoring Device market includes various methods for assessing intracranial pressure without surgical intervention. Transcranial Doppler Ultrasonography measures blood flow in the brain's arteries. Tympanic Membrane Displacement utilizes changes in ear drum movement to infer pressure levels. The Optic Nerve Sheath Diameter evaluates swelling around the optic nerve as an indicator. MRI/CT provides imaging to assess brain swelling, while Fundoscopy examines the retina for signs of papilledema, reflecting increased pressure. Each method offers unique insights into intracranial health.</p></p>
