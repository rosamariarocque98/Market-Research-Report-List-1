<p><p>Medical transcription service providers offer two primary market types: online and offline services. Online services involve real-time transcription using secure web platforms, allowing healthcare professionals to submit audio files remotely, leading to quicker turnaround times and streamlined workflows. Offline services, on the other hand, involve the collection of audio recordings which are transcribed separately and returned to the provider. This method may take longer but can be beneficial for those with limited internet access or specific data privacy concerns.</p></p>
