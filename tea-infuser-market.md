<p><p>The tea infuser market is categorized into two main types: soak once and soak multiple times. Soak once infusers are designed for single-use, allowing a tea bag or loose leaves to steep in hot water, resulting in a single brew. Conversely, soak multiple times infusers are reusable, permitting users to steep the same leaves multiple times, unlocking varying flavor profiles with each infusion. This versatility caters to different consumer preferences for both convenience and flavor exploration.</p></p>
