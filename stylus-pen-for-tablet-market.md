<p><p>The tablet stylus pen market is divided into two main types: magnetic and non-magnetic stylus pens. Magnetic stylus pens attach to the tablet using magnets, enhancing portability and preventing loss. They often offer advanced features like pressure sensitivity and tilt recognition, improving the user experience for artists and note-takers. Non-magnetic stylus pens, on the other hand, are standalone devices that do not attach magnetically. They are generally more affordable and suitable for casual users who need basic drawing or writing capabilities.</p></p>
