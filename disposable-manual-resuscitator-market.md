<p><p>The disposable manual resuscitator market comprises two main types: self-inflating resuscitators and flow-inflating resuscitators. Self-inflating resuscitators are simple devices that automatically refill with air when released, making them easy to use in emergencies. They are typically employed in pre-hospital settings. Flow-inflating resuscitators, on the other hand, require a constant gas source and allow for controlled ventilation, providing better airway management. Both types are crucial for efficient patient resuscitation in various medical scenarios.</p></p>
