<p><p>The styrenic block copolymers market primarily comprises three types: SEBS (styrene-ethylene-butylene-styrene), SIS (styrene-isoprene-styrene), and other variations. SEBS is known for its excellent elasticity and UV resistance, making it suitable for automotive and consumer goods. SIS, favored for its high clarity and flexibility, is often used in adhesives and sealants. Other types include various tailored formulations that meet specific industry requirements, enhancing applications in medical devices, packaging, and construction materials. Each type offers unique properties for diverse applications.</p></p>
