<p><ul><li>Windshield Projected HUD</li><li>Combiner Projected HUD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive Head-Up Display (HUD) market is primarily categorized into two types: Windshield Projected HUD and Combiner Projected HUD. Windshield Projected HUD projects critical information directly onto the vehicle's windshield, allowing drivers to view data like speed and navigation without diverting their gaze. In contrast, Combiner Projected HUD uses a smaller transparent screen that can be flipped up from the dashboard, displaying information closer to the driver's line of sight, enhancing safety and reducing eye movement.</p></p>
