<p><p>MgO boards, or magnesium oxide boards, are categorized based on thickness into three market types: Thin (below 8 mm), Medium (8-15 mm), and Thick (above 15 mm). Thin MgO boards are often used for lightweight applications like interior partitions and wall panels. Medium boards serve versatile construction needs, including flooring and roofing, offering a balance of strength and weight. Thick boards provide enhanced durability and insulation, making them ideal for exterior cladding and high-performance building solutions.</p></p>
