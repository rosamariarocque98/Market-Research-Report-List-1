<p><p>The Semiconductor Gate Valve market is categorized into two main types based on connection methods: flanged connection and threaded connection. Flanged connection valves feature a flat surface with holes for bolts, allowing for secure attachment to pipelines, facilitating ease of maintenance and robust sealing. Threaded connection valves utilize male and female threads for secure attachment, making them easy to install and remove. Each type serves distinct applications in semiconductor manufacturing, where precision and reliability in fluid control are critical.</p></p>
