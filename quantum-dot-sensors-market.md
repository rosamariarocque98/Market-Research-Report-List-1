<p><ul><li>Resistance Strain Quantum Dot Sensors</li><li>Piezoresistive Quantum Dot Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The quantum dot sensors market primarily includes resistance strain quantum dot sensors and piezoresistive quantum dot sensors. Resistance strain quantum dot sensors utilize the change in electrical resistance that occurs when a material is deformed, making them ideal for monitoring structural integrity. In contrast, piezoresistive quantum dot sensors exploit the piezoresistive effect, where resistance changes in response to mechanical stress, offering high sensitivity in applications like biomedical devices and wearable technology. Both types promise enhanced performance across various industries.</p></p>
