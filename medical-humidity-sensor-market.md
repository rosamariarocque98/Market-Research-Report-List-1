<p><ul><li>Relative Humidity Sensor</li><li>Absolute Humidity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical humidity sensor market comprises two primary types: relative humidity sensors and absolute humidity sensors. Relative humidity sensors measure the percentage of moisture in the air relative to the maximum amount the air can hold at a given temperature, making them crucial for environments like hospitals where humidity control is vital for patient comfort and equipment performance. Absolute humidity sensors, on the other hand, measure the actual water vapor content in the air, providing precise data essential for various medical applications.</p></p>
