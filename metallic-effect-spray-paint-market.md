<p><p>Metallic effect spray paints are used to create a shiny, reflective finish resembling metal. The market is divided into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based paints contain volatile organic compounds (VOCs) that evaporate quickly, offering durability and a high-gloss finish, ideal for automotive and industrial applications. Water-based paints are eco-friendly, with lower VOC levels, making them safer for indoor use and environmentally conscious projects. Both types provide metallic effects but differ in application, drying time, and environmental impact.</p></p>
