<p><p>The automotive air conditioning filter market includes two primary types: particle filters and activated carbon filters. Particle filters effectively trap dust, pollen, and other airborne particles, ensuring clean air circulation within the vehicle. Activated carbon filters go a step further by adsorbing odors, harmful gases, and volatile organic compounds, enhancing overall air quality. Both filters contribute to passenger comfort and health by reducing allergens and unpleasant smells, making them essential components in modern automotive air conditioning systems.</p></p>
