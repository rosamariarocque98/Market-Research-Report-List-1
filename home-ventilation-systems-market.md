<p><strong>The Home Ventilation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exhaust Ventilation Systems</li><li>Supply Ventilation Systems</li><li>Balanced Ventilation Systems</li><li>Energy Recovery Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Home ventilation systems are essential for maintaining indoor air quality and comfort. Exhaust ventilation systems remove stale air from indoor spaces, creating negative pressure that draws in fresh air. Supply ventilation systems introduce outdoor air into the home, promoting airflow and reducing moisture buildup. Balanced ventilation systems maintain equal airflow in and out, optimizing indoor conditions. Energy recovery systems enhance efficiency by transferring heat and moisture between incoming and outgoing air, minimizing energy loss while ensuring a healthy living environment.</p></p>
