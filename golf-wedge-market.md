<p><p>The golf wedge market is categorized into four main types: Pitching Wedge, Gap Wedge, Sand Wedge, and Lob Wedge. The Pitching Wedge is designed for mid-distance shots and approach play. The Gap Wedge fills the distance void between pitching and sand wedges, ideal for short approaches. The Sand Wedge is specialized for bunker play, featuring a wider sole for easier sand interaction. The Lob Wedge allows for high, short shots with maximum loft, enabling precise control around the greens.</p></p>
