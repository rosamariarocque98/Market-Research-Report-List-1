<p><p>A suprapubic catheter is a medical device used to drain urine from the bladder through a small incision made in the abdomen, just above the pubic bone. This method is typically employed when traditional urethral catheters are inappropriate or ineffective, such as in cases of urethral blockage or after certain surgical procedures.</p><p>The suprapubic catheter market is experiencing robust growth, driven by increasing incidences of urinary retention, prostate surgeries, and the rise in chronic urinary conditions. Hospitals are emphasizing the use of less invasive methods, further promoting the adoption of suprapubic catheters. The growing geriatric population, who often face issues related to urinary incontinence, is expected to fuel demand in the coming years. Technological advancements, including the development of safer and more comfortable catheter designs, play a crucial role in market expansion. </p><p>The suprapubic catheter market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 14.4% during the forecast period.
