<p><p>The Purification UV Sensors market is segmented into three main types: UVA, UVB, and UVC sensors. UVA sensors primarily detect long-wavelength ultraviolet light, often used in applications like water treatment and air purification. UVB sensors focus on medium-wavelength UV light and are commonly utilized in skincare and dermatology for monitoring exposure levels. UVC sensors, which target short-wavelength UV light, are crucial for disinfection processes in medical and industrial settings, effectively killing pathogens and enhancing safety in various environments.</p></p>
