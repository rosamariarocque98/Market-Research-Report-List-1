<p><p>The market for fuses in electrical equipment and electronic products features several key types: filamentous, flake, glass tube, and ceramic tube fuses. Filamentous fuses contain a thin wire element that melts to interrupt current flow. Flake fuses utilize a metal strip that melts under excessive heat. Glass tube fuses provide visible protection and are often used in consumer electronics, while ceramic tube fuses offer higher temperature resistance and durability, making them ideal for industrial applications. Each type serves specific needs in protecting electrical circuits.</p></p>
