<p><strong>The PoE Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bullet Type</li><li>Dome Type</li><li>PTZ Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PoE camera market comprises three primary types: Bullet, Dome, and PTZ cameras. Bullet cameras are cylindrical and ideal for outdoor use due to their weather-resistant design, offering long-range visibility. Dome cameras, encased in a dome-shaped housing, are often used in indoor settings for discreet surveillance and are less prone to vandalism. PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) cameras provide dynamic monitoring with adjustable angles and zoom capabilities, making them suitable for large areas requiring real-time tracking and versatile surveillance solutions.</p></p>
