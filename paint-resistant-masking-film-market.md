<p><p>The Paint Resistant Masking Film market is divided into two primary types: Solid Paint Resistant Masking Film and Liquid Paint Resistant Masking Film. Solid films are used for surface protection during painting, providing a robust barrier against overspray and residue, making them ideal for commercial and industrial applications. In contrast, Liquid films are applied as a spray or coating, forming a protective layer that can be easily peeled off post-application, suitable for both professional and DIY projects. Each type serves distinct needs in paint protection.</p></p>
