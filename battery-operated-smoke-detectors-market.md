<p><p>The battery-operated smoke detector market includes various types, primarily categorized into photoelectric and ionization models. Photoelectric smoke detectors use light beams to sense smoke, providing faster responses to smoldering fires, making them ideal for residential areas. Conversely, ionization smoke detectors utilize radioactive material to detect fast-flaming fires, offering quicker alerts for different fire scenarios. Additionally, the market includes other specialized smoke detectors, incorporating features like carbon monoxide detection or smart technology, catering to diverse consumer needs and safety preferences.</p></p>
