<p><p>The motor protection market comprises several key types that safeguard electric motors from damage. Overload relays monitor motor current and prevent overheating by disconnecting power when excessive current is detected. Vacuum contactors enable high voltage switching with minimal arcing, improving durability. Combination starters integrate these functions, providing both motor starting and protection in a single unit. Motor protection circuit breakers combine overload protection and short circuit protection, ensuring comprehensive safeguarding against electrical faults in motor applications.</p></p>
