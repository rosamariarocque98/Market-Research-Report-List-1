<p><strong>The Composite Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Autoclave</li><li>Out-of-autoclave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The composite film market is primarily categorized into autoclave and out-of-autoclave processes. Autoclave technology involves curing composite materials under high pressure and temperature in a sealed chamber, ensuring high performance and structural integrity, commonly used in aerospace and automotive industries. In contrast, out-of-autoclave methods utilize lower pressures and can employ vacuum bagging or other techniques, offering versatility and cost-effectiveness. This approach is gaining popularity in sectors like wind energy and consumer goods, enabling efficient large-scale production.</p></p>
