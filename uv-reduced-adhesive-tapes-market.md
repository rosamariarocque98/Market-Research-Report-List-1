<p><p>The UV-reduced adhesive tapes market is categorized into single-sided and double-sided types. Single-sided adhesive tapes feature a layer of adhesive on one side, making them ideal for applications like bonding, mounting, and sealing where one surface needs adhesion. Double-sided adhesive tapes, on the other hand, have adhesive on both sides, allowing for secure attachment between two surfaces without visible tape. Both types are widely used across industries for their efficiency and adaptability in various bonding applications.</p></p>
